The Best Movies According to Ethan

My top 3 movies

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

Probably is my favorite movie to this date and has a third part in the works. This movie is about the coming up of Miles Morales and how his life changed after being bit but due to him being bit it changed the path he was supposed to follow and ended up leading to the death of his mentor Peter Parker. The plot then expands on how the main villian is trying to open a portal to other dimensions which leads to multiple Spidermen being brought to his universe but int heir own style as the art of this movie is superb.

How to Train Your Dragon

This movie is about the friendship between a dragon and a viking that are supposed to be at odds with each other but due to an injury the dragon can't live on his own anymore. The viking Hiccup is the son of the Chief of Berk and hasn't killed a dragon which leads to a disparity in the village about his status, but proves himself through the friendship and by learning about his friends what they're like.

Deadpool

This movie is an action-comedy about the anti-hero Deadpool and his origin. While the character is raunchy if your humor is suited it, the movie is for you as the persona of Deadpool is about breaking the fourth-wall and doing everything his way which makes for a very entertaining watch.
