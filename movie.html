The Best Movies According to Ethan

My Top 3 Movies

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

My favorite movie to date. It follows Miles Morales as he becomes Spider-Man, leading to unexpected consequences and the expansion of a multiverse story.

How to Train Your Dragon

A story about the friendship between a Viking and a dragon, showing how understanding others can be more powerful than conflict.

Deadpool

An action-comedy featuring an anti-hero known for breaking the fourth wall and delivering a unique, humorous experience.
